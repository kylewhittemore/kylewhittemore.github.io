My Coding Journey

As a young person, I found coding in fifth grade. I had a saavy science teacher, who would let me play around programming in Basic on an apple computer. In high school I took courses in Visual Basic and C++. Throughout the bulk of my adult life I didn't give coding another thought, until one day I did. Ever since then I have pursued coding as a passion and a skill that I hope to develop into a career.
